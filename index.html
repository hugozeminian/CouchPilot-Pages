The difference

Why not the other remote apps?

Many remote apps are built around ads, subscriptions, accounts, and tracking. Banner ads between button presses, paywalls to unlock volume, and analytics watching every tap.

CouchPilot is the opposite by design — and it only claims to support what it actually supports.

How it works

Three steps, all in your home

1

Same Wi-Fi

Your phone and TV join the same home network. That's the only requirement.

2

Pair once

Your TV shows a prompt — accept it once. The pairing stays on your phone, in secure storage.

3

Control locally

Every button press goes straight from phone to TV. No servers in the middle, nothing to sign into.
