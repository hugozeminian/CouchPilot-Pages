Roadmap

Where CouchPilot is going

Direction, not dates. We add a TV system only after it's proven on real hardware — so this roadmap grows honestly rather than by promise.

Now LG webOS Supported

  • Full remote, apps, inputs, touchpad, keyboard, Wi-Fi wake, auto-reconnect.
  • My Devices multi-TV switching.
  • Owner-validated on real hardware.

Now Samsung Tizen Experimental

  • Pairing, remote, volume/mute, popular-app shortcuts, input (Source), Wi-Fi wake on supported models.
  • Included and clearly labeled experimental; features vary by model.
  • Growing through real-world feedback.

Next Android TV / Google TV Planned

  • Covers many Sony, TCL, Hisense, Philips, Xiaomi TVs plus Google TV Streamer and NVIDIA Shield.
  • In active development; not selectable yet.

Research Roku Planned

  • Under research. Roku's own settings can restrict third-party remotes, so support will depend on device and settings.

Evaluating Vizio & others Under review

  • Vizio (SmartCast / Vizio OS) is being evaluated. Other systems are assessed as demand and feasibility warrant.

Ongoing Quality & compatibility

  • Growing the honest, model-level compatibility list from user reports.
  • Android alongside iOS.
  • Refinements to reliability, accessibility, and the control surface.

Everything here stays local-first: no account, no ads, no tracking — on every platform we add. Any future paid extras would be optional, and the core remote stays free.
