Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026 · Contact: app.hugocamargo@gmail.com

The short version

CouchPilot is a local-first TV remote. It talks only to your TV on your local Wi-Fi network. It does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data. There is no account, no ads, and no third-party analytics or tracking.

What we collect

Nothing automatically. The app has no servers and no backend, and it never sends us information about you or your usage on its own. The only way we receive anything is if you choose to email us feedback (see "Optional feedback you choose to send" below) — and then only what you decide to send.

What stays on your device

  • Your TV's address and a pairing credential (used to reconnect without re-prompting) are stored only on your phone, in the operating system's secure storage. They are never transmitted to us or to any third party, and the app never displays the pairing credential.
  • Your in-app preferences (such as favorites and pointer speed) are stored locally on your phone.

You can clear all of this at any time with Reset pairing in the app, or by deleting the app.

Optional feedback you choose to send

The app has a "Compatibility & feedback" screen you can use to tell us whether it works with your TV. This is entirely optional and always initiated by you: the app prepares a message and opens your own email app or share sheet so you can review and send it yourself — the app has no server and sends nothing on its own. You see the exact contents before sending. The message includes only the app version, your TV brand/model, connection status, and any notes you type; it never includes your network address (IP), your TV's hardware address (MAC), or any pairing credential. If you email us, we receive whatever you chose to send, in our email inbox — the same as any email you send us directly. We do not use it for tracking or advertising.

Network access

The app communicates directly with your TV over your home network (Wi-Fi/LAN). This local connection is required for a remote to work. The app does not send your data over the internet.

On iOS, the app requests permission to access devices on your local network (required to reach your TV). On Android 13+, it may request the nearby Wi-Fi devices permission for the same reason. These permissions are used only to connect to your TV on your network. The app does not request location, contacts, microphone, or camera access.

Children

The app is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

Third parties

The app contains no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. No data is shared with third parties because no data is collected.

Changes

If this ever changes (for example, if a future optional feature needs different handling), we will update this policy and the app's store privacy disclosures before the change ships.
