Report whether a device works

The fastest, most useful report comes straight from the app:

  • Open CouchPilot → Compatibility & feedback (home screen, My Devices, or Diagnostics).
  • Pick Worked / Partly / Didn't work.
  • Add a note describing what happened.
  • Send by email or share — you review the exact text first.

Reporting a bug — what to include

  • Your TV brand and exact model number (from the box or the TV's About screen) — this matters more than the brand alone.
  • Your TV's firmware/software version if you know it.
  • What you tried and what happened (which buttons worked, which didn't).
  • Whether your phone and TV were on the same Wi-Fi network.
  • A screenshot if it helps show the issue.

Privacy note: the in-app report shows you the exact text before you send it. It never includes your IP address, MAC address, pairing tokens, or any key — those are removed automatically. You choose whether to send anything at all.

Requirements

  • iPhone (iOS) — Android coming later.
  • Your phone and TV on the same Wi-Fi / local network.
  • A supported TV system — see Supported Devices.
