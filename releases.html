v1.0.0 First release In preparation

The initial CouchPilot release, currently being validated before store submission.

  • Full LG webOS remote: power, volume, navigation, apps, inputs, touchpad, keyboard, Wi-Fi wake.
  • Automatic reconnect when you open the app.
  • My Devices — save and switch between multiple TVs.
  • Experimental Samsung (Tizen) support, clearly labeled; features vary by model.
  • Compatibility & feedback — private, no account, nothing sent unless you choose.
  • Local-first: no ads, no account, no tracking.
